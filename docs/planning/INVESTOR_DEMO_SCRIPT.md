# LearnX MVP Demo Script

## Positioning

LearnX is a supportive Study AI for all learners, with exam preparation as the clearest first wedge.

The demo should show one measurable loop:

**Ask -> Diagnose weakness -> Targeted drill -> Recovery score**

## 3-Minute Flow

1. Start on `/app`.
   - Say: "The product opens on today's study loop, not a generic dashboard."
   - Type a question into the main Study AI box.

2. Move to `/app/ask`.
   - Show the tutor answer.
   - Point to the weakness diagnosis.
   - Say: "LearnX does not stop at an explanation. It identifies what to check next."

3. Click `Start targeted drill`.
   - Answer the drill.
   - Submit it through the server-scored flow.

4. Open `/app/practice/results`.
   - Show score, weak concepts, and recovery score.
   - Say: "This is the measurable learning moment: the student can see what recovered and what still needs repair."

5. Refresh.
   - Confirm the result remains in history.
   - Say: "Local cache keeps the app fast, but authenticated backend scoring and sync are the source of truth."

## What To Avoid In The Demo

- Do not browse every subject.
- Do not open every sidebar panel.
- Do not frame LearnX as a chatbot.
- Do not lead with rewards, notes, or search.

## One-Line Close

LearnX turns a student question into a diagnosed practice loop, then proves progress with a recovery score.
